## 🛡️ **Materials & Craftsmanship**

### **Precious Metals**

#### **Sterling Silver (925)**
- **Composition**: 92.5% pure silver, 7.5% alloy metals
- **Properties**: Bright white luster, durable, hypoallergenic
- **Care**: Tarnishes naturally, polish regularly
- **Hallmark**: "925" stamped on all pieces
- **Benefits**: Affordable luxury, classic appeal

#### **Gold-Plated Silver**
- **Base**: 925 sterling silver core
- **Plating**: 2-3 microns of 18k gold
- **Durability**: With proper care, plating lasts 2-3 years
- **Replating**: Available through Norelle service centers
- **Benefits**: Gold appearance at silver price point

#### **18k Gold**
- **Purity**: 75% pure gold, 25% alloy metals
- **Colors**: Available in yellow, white, and rose gold
- **Properties**: Luxurious, durable, hypoallergenic
- **Hallmark**: "18k" stamped on all pieces
- **Investment**: Retains value over time

### **Gemstones**

#### **Diamonds**
- **Quality Standards**: G-H color, SI1-SI2 clarity minimum
- **Certification**: GIA certified for stones 0.5ct and above
- **Sourcing**: Conflict-free, Kimberley Process compliant
- **Cut**: Brilliant cut for maximum sparkle
- **Care**: Professional cleaning recommended

#### **Pearls**
- **Type**: Freshwater cultured pearls
- **Grade**: AA quality with high luster
- **Shape**: Near-round to baroque
- **Size**: 6-8mm average
- **Origin**: Sustainably farmed
- **Care**: Organic gem requiring special attention

#### **Lab-Created Gemstones**
- **Benefits**: Ethical, affordable, identical properties
- **Types**: Rubies, sapphires, emeralds available
- **Quality**: Same chemical composition as natural stones
- **Durability**: Often more durable than natural counterparts
- **Environmental**: Reduced mining impact

## 🛠️ **Care & Maintenance**

### **Daily Care**

#### **Wearing Guidelines**
- **Last On, First Off**: Apply cosmetics before jewelry
- **Avoid Water**: Remove before swimming, showering, or hot tubs
- **Chemical Exposure**: Avoid perfumes, lotions, hairspray
- **Physical Activity**: Remove during sports or exercise
- **Storage**: Store separately to prevent scratching

#### **Cleaning Routine**
- **After Each Wear**: Wipe with soft, dry cloth
- **Weekly Cleaning**: Mild soap and water solution
- **Monthly Deep Clean**: Jewelry cleaning solution
- **Professional Cleaning**: Every 6-12 months
- **Inspection**: Check for loose stones or damage

### **Material-Specific Care**

#### **Sterling Silver**
- **Tarnish Prevention**: Store in anti-tarnish pouches
- **Cleaning**: Silver polishing cloth for regular maintenance
- **Deep Cleaning**: Silver dip solution (follow instructions)
- **Avoid**: Rubber bands (can cause tarnish)
- **Storage**: Airtight containers when not wearing

#### **Gold-Plated Items**
- **Gentle Cleaning**: Soft cloth only, no abrasives
- **Avoid**: Harsh chemicals, abrasive materials
- **Water**: Limit water exposure
- **Replating**: Every 2-3 years for wear
- **Storage**: Soft pouch to prevent scratching

#### **Pearls & Organic Gems**
- **Special Care**: Wipe with damp cloth after wear
- **Storage**: Separate from other jewelry
- **Avoid**: Ultrasonic cleaners, steam cleaning
- **Restringing**: Every 2-3 years for pearl strands
- **Chemicals**: Extremely sensitive to acids

#### **Diamonds & Hard Stones**
- **Regular Cleaning**: Jewelry cleaning solution
- **Professional Care**: Annual inspection and cleaning
- **Settings**: Check prongs and settings regularly
- **Storage**: Can be stored with other hard gems
- **Insurance**: Consider insurance for valuable pieces

### **Storage Solutions**

#### **Individual Storage**
- **Jewelry Boxes**: Compartments for different pieces
- **Pouches**: Soft fabric pouches for travel
- **Hanging Storage**: For necklaces to prevent tangling
- **Anti-Tarnish**: Specialized storage for silver

#### **Travel Tips**
- **Travel Case**: Dedicated jewelry travel case
- **Packing**: Roll delicate items in soft cloth
- **Carry-On**: Keep valuable jewelry in carry-on luggage
- **Insurance**: Check travel insurance coverage

## ❓ **Frequently Asked Questions**

### **Product Questions**

#### **Q: Are your materials hypoallergenic?**
**A**: Yes, all Norelle jewelry is nickel-free and lead-free. We use 925 sterling silver and 18k gold, which are hypoallergenic for most people. If you have severe metal allergies, contact us for custom options.

#### **Q: Do you use real diamonds and gemstones?**
**A**: We offer both natural and lab-created gemstones. All our diamonds are conflict-free and Kimberley Process compliant. Lab-created stones have identical chemical properties to natural stones but are more ethical and affordable.

#### **Q: How do I know my ring size?**
**A**: We recommend professional sizing at a Norelle boutique. For home sizing, use our printable ring sizer or measure with a string. Consider sizing up 1/4 size for wider bands.

#### **Q: Can I shower with my jewelry on?**
**A**: We recommend removing jewelry before showering. Water, soap, and shampoo can damage delicate pieces and cause premature tarnishing on silver.

### **Care Questions**

#### **Q: How often should I clean my jewelry?**
**A**: Wipe with a soft cloth after each wear. Clean with mild soap and water weekly. Professional cleaning every 6-12 months maintains optimal condition.

#### **Q: Why is my silver jewelry tarnishing?**
**A**: Tarnish is a natural reaction between silver and sulfur in the air. Regular cleaning and proper storage in anti-tarnish pouches minimizes tarnishing.

#### **Q: Can I use ultrasonic cleaners?**
**A**: Ultrasonic cleaners are safe for diamonds and hard gemstones but should never be used for pearls, opals, or other organic gems.

#### **Q: How do I store my jewelry?**
**A**: Store each piece separately in a soft pouch or jewelry box compartment. Keep silver in anti-tarnish storage and avoid direct sunlight.

### **Order & Service Questions**

#### **Q: Do you offer international shipping?**
**A**: Yes, we ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

#### **Q: Can I return or exchange my jewelry?**
**A**: We offer 30-day returns on unworn items in original packaging. Custom and engraved pieces are final sale unless there's a manufacturing defect.

#### **Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?**
**A**: Yes, complimentary gift wrapping is available on all orders. Add a personalized message at checkout for the complete gift experience.

#### **Q: How long does shipping take?**
**A**: Standard shipping: 5-7 business days. Express shipping: 2-3 business days. International shipping: 10-15 business days.
