## 💰 Cost Structure Analysis

### **Mass-Market Luxury Brands**
- **Fragrance Juice**: 3% of total cost
- **Packaging**: 10-20% of total cost
- **Marketing**: Up to 80% of retail price
- **Retail Markup**: 3-7x manufacturing cost

**Example Breakdown ($100 bottle)**:
- Manufacturing: $10
- Marketing: $10
- Packaging: $5
- Distribution: $3
- Brand Profit + Retail Markup: $72

### **Niche Luxury Brands**
- **High-Quality Ingredients**: 40-60% of production cost
- **Craftsmanship**: Significant labor cost component
- **Rare Materials**: Oud ($50,000-80,000/liter)
- **Concentration**: 20-40% fragrance oil (vs 2-4% mass market)

## 🎨 Price Tier Strategy

### **Entry-Level Luxury ($50-$100)**
**Target**: First-time luxury buyers
- **Position**: Accessible luxury gateway
- **Volume**: High sales volume potential
- **Margin**: Moderate (3-4x cost)
- **Examples**: 30ml bottles, Eau de Toilette

### **Core Luxury ($100-$200)**
**Target**: Established luxury consumers
- **Position**: Main product line
- **Volume**: Balanced sales volume
- **Margin**: Good (4-6x cost)
- **Examples**: 50-100ml Eau de Parfum

### **Premium Luxury ($200-$350)**
**Target**: Affluent collectors
- **Position**: Premium offerings
- **Volume**: Lower volume, higher value
- **Margin**: Excellent (6-8x cost)
- **Examples**: Extrait de Parfum, limited editions

### **Ultra-Luxury ($350+)**
**Target**: Ultra-high net worth
- **Position**: Brand prestige builders
- **Volume**: Very limited
- **Margin**: Exceptional (8-15x cost)
- **Examples**: Bespoke, exclusive editions

## 🔍 Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### **Key Risks**
1. **Price Resistance**: New brand at premium pricing
   - **Mitigation**: Quality storytelling, sample programs
2. **Competition**: Established luxury brands
   - **Mitigation**: Unique positioning, heritage emphasis
3. **Cost Inflation**: Raw material price volatility
   - **Mitigation**: Long-term supplier contracts, hedging
4. **Currency Fluctuation**: International pricing impact
   - **Mitigation**: Regional pricing strategies, currency hedging

### **Success Factors**
- **Quality Consistency**: Maintain niche-level ingredient quality
- **Brand Storytelling**: Compelling Belgian heritage narrative
- **Customer Experience**: Luxury service at every touchpoint
- **Strategic Distribution**: Careful partner selection
